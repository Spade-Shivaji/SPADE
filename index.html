Society for Practical Applications & Development of Economics

Society for Practical Applications and Development of Economics (SPADE) is the Socio-Economic society of Shivaji College, University of Delhi. It was founded as a club in 2016 by a group of students who believed that a platform was needed to shed light on various socio-economic issues to broaden students' understanding of them.SPADE was given the stature of society in 2017. The main objective of SPADE is to create an economic school of thought where the views of experts, teachers, and students are taken into account in order to determine how we, as the nation's youth, may contribute to redefining the preconceived beliefs.

Annual Consortium
of Excellence

The Annual Consortium of Excellence (ACE), also known as ACE of SPADE, is celebrated every year and students from different universities and colleges spread across Delhi-NCR, participate in large numbers in various fun-filled events and exhilarating games. Our team has prepared some awe-thrilling and exciting events to ensure a memorable experience for participants.

Annual Documentaries
